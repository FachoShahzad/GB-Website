Tourist Destinations of Gilgit Baltistan

Gilgit Baltistan has 14 Different Districts with different cultures and traditions.

Gilgit

LAND OF DIVERSITY

District Gilgit, capital of Gilgit-Baltistan, is strategically the most important territory in the Karakorum Range as it has been the trade center of the area.

Astore

LAND OF PASTURE

Astore District is one of the ten districts in Gilgit−Baltistan, with its administrative centers located in Eidgah and Gorikot. To the north, it borders Gilgit.

Skardu

LAND OF PEACE

District Skardu in Baltistan Division is located at the confluence of Indus River and Shayok River that leads toward the legendary tourist destination of the region.

LAND OF BEAUTY
